Back Exercises

Build a strong, wide, and powerful back with proper technique.

Health Tools
Real Human

01. Pull-Up

Builds back width and grip strength.

Steps
  1. Grip bar shoulder-width
  2. Pull chest toward bar
  3. Lower slowly
Safety
  • Avoid swinging
  • Control full movement
Real Human

02. Lat Pulldown

Develops wide lats.

Steps
  1. Pull bar to chest
  2. Keep chest up
  3. Control upward return
Safety
  • Do not pull behind neck
  • Keep movement smooth
Real Human

03. Barbell Row

Builds thickness and strength.

Steps
  1. Bend at hips
  2. Pull bar to torso
  3. Lower under control
Safety
  • Keep back straight
  • Avoid jerking weight
Real Human

04. Deadlift

Full posterior chain strength.

Steps
  1. Keep spine neutral
  2. Lift with legs and hips
  3. Stand tall
Safety
  • Never round back
  • Start light
