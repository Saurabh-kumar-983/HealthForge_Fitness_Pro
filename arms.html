Arms Exercises

Build biceps, triceps, and forearms with precision and control.

Health Tools
Real Human

01. Barbell Curl

Builds biceps size and strength.

Steps
  1. Keep elbows fixed
  2. Curl bar slowly upward
  3. Squeeze at top
Safety
  • Avoid swinging
  • Keep wrists neutral
Real Human

02. Hammer Curl

Forearm and brachialis strength.

Steps
  1. Hold neutral grip
  2. Lift dumbbells evenly
  3. Lower with control
Safety
  • Do not swing
  • Keep elbows close
Real Human

03. Triceps Pushdown

Triceps isolation and strength.

Steps
  1. Keep elbows fixed
  2. Push down fully
  3. Return slowly
Safety
  • Avoid shoulder movement
  • Control full range
Real Human

04. Skull Crusher

Triceps mass builder.

Steps
  1. Lower bar toward forehead
  2. Keep elbows steady
  3. Extend arms back up
Safety
  • Use light weight first
  • Do not flare elbows
