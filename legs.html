🦵 Body Part Training

Legs Exercises

Train with control, build strength, and protect your joints.

Health Tools
Real Human

01. Squat

Builds total lower body strength.

Steps
  1. Stand shoulder-width apart
  2. Push hips back and bend knees
  3. Keep chest up and spine neutral
  4. Drive up through heels
Safety
  • Avoid knees collapsing inward
  • Keep back straight
  • Start with bodyweight first
Real Human

02. Leg Press

Safe heavy leg training.

Steps
  1. Place feet shoulder-width on platform
  2. Lower slowly
  3. Push through heels
Safety
  • Do not lock knees
  • Keep lower back pressed
Real Human

03. Walking Lunge

Balance + strength combo.

Steps
  1. Step forward into lunge
  2. Lower until both knees bent
  3. Push forward into next step
Safety
  • Keep torso upright
  • Avoid knee overextension
Real Human

04. Romanian Deadlift

Hamstrings & glutes.

Steps
  1. Keep slight knee bend
  2. Push hips back
  3. Lower bar along legs
Safety
  • Do not round back
  • Keep core tight
