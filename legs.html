01. Squat

Benefit: Quads, glutes, and total strength.

Steps to perform
  1. Stand or sit with feet stable and knees tracking over toes.
  2. Brace your core and keep the spine neutral.
  3. Lower with control through a comfortable range.
  4. Drive through the full foot and finish tall without jerking.
Safety points
  • Warm up first and start with a light set.
  • Use a spotter or safety pins for heavy sets.
  • Stop if you feel sharp pain, dizziness, or joint discomfort.
  • Keep breathing, avoid holding your breath for long periods.

04. Romanian Deadlift

Benefit: Hamstrings and glutes.

Steps to perform
  1. Start with a neutral spine and engaged lats.
  2. Pull using the back, not only the arms.
  3. Pause briefly at peak contraction.
  4. Return slowly without rounding the lower back.
Safety points
  • Warm up first and start with a light set.
  • Use a spotter or safety pins for heavy sets.
  • Stop if you feel sharp pain, dizziness, or joint discomfort.

06. Leg Curl

Benefit: Hamstring strength.

Steps to perform
  1. Lock the upper arm position before starting.
  2. Move only through the working joint with slow control.
  3. Squeeze at the top without swinging.
  4. Lower fully while keeping wrists neutral.
Safety points
  • Warm up first and start with a light set.
  • Stop if you feel sharp pain, dizziness, or joint discomfort.
  • Keep breathing, avoid holding your breath for long periods.

07. Calf Raise

Benefit: Calves and ankle strength.

Steps to perform
  1. Brace the abdomen before movement starts.
  2. Keep ribs down and pelvis controlled.
  3. Move slowly enough to avoid momentum.
  4. Stop the set when form starts breaking.
Safety points
