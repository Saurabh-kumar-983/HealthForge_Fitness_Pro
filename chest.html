01. Barbell Bench Press

Benefit: Overall chest size and pressing strength.

Steps to perform
  1. Set shoulder blades back and down before lifting.
  2. Grip evenly, brace your core, and keep feet planted.
  3. Lower under control until the target muscle is stretched.
  4. Press smoothly without bouncing or locking joints aggressively.
Safety points
  • Warm up first and start with a light set.
  • Use a spotter or safety pins for heavy sets.
  • Stop if you feel sharp pain, dizziness, or joint discomfort.
  • Keep breathing, avoid holding your breath for long periods.
