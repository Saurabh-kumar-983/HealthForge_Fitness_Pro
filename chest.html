Chest Exercises

Build strength, size, and control with proper technique.

Health Tools
Real Human

01. Barbell Bench Press

Builds chest size and strength.

Steps
  1. Lie flat and grip bar slightly wider
  2. Lower to chest slowly
  3. Press upward with control
Safety
  • Use spotter for heavy lifts
  • Do not bounce bar
Real Human

02. Incline Dumbbell Press

Upper chest development.

Steps
  1. Set bench incline
  2. Lower dumbbells slowly
  3. Press upward evenly
Safety
  • Keep control
  • Avoid shoulder strain
Real Human

03. Push-Up

Bodyweight chest strength.

Steps
  1. Keep body straight
  2. Lower chest to floor
  3. Push back up
Safety
  • Do not sag hips
  • Keep elbows controlled
