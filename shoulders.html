Shoulders Strength

01. Overhead Press

Benefit: Shoulder power and full upper-body strength.

Steps
  1. Stand firm with core tight
  2. Press bar overhead in straight line
  3. Keep elbows slightly forward
  4. Lower slowly with control
Safety
  • Do not arch lower back
  • Avoid locking elbows hard
  • Start with light weight
Real Human

02. Lateral Raise

Builds wider shoulders.

Steps
  1. Lift arms sideways to shoulder level
  2. Keep slight bend in elbows
  3. Control the descent
Safety
  • Avoid swinging weights
  • Use light dumbbells
Real Human

03. Front Raise

Front delt strength.

Steps
  1. Raise weights to eye level
  2. Keep core tight
  3. Lower slowly
Safety
  • Avoid jerky motion
  • Keep posture upright
