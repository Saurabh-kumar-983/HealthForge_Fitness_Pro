01. Overhead Press

Benefit: Shoulder power and full upper-body strength.

Steps to perform
  1. Set up with balanced stance and correct equipment height.
  2. Brace your core and align the joints before moving.
  3. Perform each repetition slowly with full control.
  4. Return to the start position without losing posture.
Safety points
  • Warm up first and start with a light set.
  • Avoid forcing range if the shoulder feels pinched.
  • Use a spotter or safety pins for heavy sets.
  • Stop if you feel sharp pain, dizziness, or joint discomfort.

02. Lateral Raise

Benefit: Wider shoulders and side delt development.

Steps to perform
  1. Brace the abdomen before movement starts.
  2. Keep ribs down and pelvis controlled.
  3. Move slowly enough to avoid momentum.
  4. Stop the set when form starts breaking.
Safety points
  • Warm up first and start with a light set.
  • Stop if you feel sharp pain, dizziness, or joint discomfort.
  • Keep breathing, avoid holding your breath for long periods.

05. Arnold Press

Benefit: All-round shoulder development.

Steps to perform
  1. Set shoulder blades back and down before lifting.
  2. Grip evenly, brace your core, and keep feet planted.
  3. Lower under control until the target muscle is stretched.
  4. Press smoothly without bouncing or locking joints aggressively.
Safety points

07. Upright Row

Benefit: Side delts and upper traps.

Steps to perform
  1. Start with a neutral spine and engaged lats.
  2. Pull using the back, not only the arms.
  3. Pause briefly at peak contraction.
  4. Return slowly without rounding the lower back.
Safety points
  • Warm up first and start with a light set.
  • Avoid forcing range if the shoulder feels pinched.
  • Stop if you feel sharp pain, dizziness, or joint discomfort.
