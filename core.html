Core Exercises

Build a strong, stable core with controlled movement and proper posture.

Health Tools
Real Human

01. Plank

Core stability and endurance.

Steps
  1. Keep body straight from head to heels
  2. Engage core and glutes
  3. Hold position without sagging
Safety
  • Avoid dropping hips
  • Do not hold breath
Real Human

02. Crunch

Upper abs activation.

Steps
  1. Lie flat with knees bent
  2. Lift shoulders slightly
  3. Contract abs and return
Safety
  • Do not pull neck
  • Keep movement controlled
Real Human

03. Leg Raise

Lower abs focus.

Steps
  1. Keep legs straight
  2. Lift slowly
  3. Lower without touching ground
Safety
  • Avoid arching back
  • Control movement fully
Real Human

04. Russian Twist

Obliques and rotation.

Steps
  1. Lean back slightly
  2. Rotate torso side to side
  3. Keep core tight
Safety
  • Do not rush movement
  • Avoid spine twisting forcefully
