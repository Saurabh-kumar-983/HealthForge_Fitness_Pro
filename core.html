01. Plank

Benefit: Core endurance and stability.

Steps to perform
  1. Brace the abdomen before movement starts.
  2. Keep ribs down and pelvis controlled.
  3. Move slowly enough to avoid momentum.
  4. Stop the set when form starts breaking.
Safety points
  • Warm up first and start with a light set.
  • Stop if you feel sharp pain, dizziness, or joint discomfort.
  • Keep breathing, avoid holding your breath for long periods.
